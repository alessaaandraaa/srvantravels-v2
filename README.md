Welcome to the SR Van Travels website! This website is a prototype for SRVan Travels, a local Cebu travel agency providing van services to tourists who want to explore Cebu.

# FEATURES

## Package Booking
A collection of packages provided by SRVan Travels*, complete with all needed details. Users are to simply input their booking details.

*Data in the website is not reflective of the actual packages provided by SRVan Travels.

## Custom Itinerary Booking
A unique feature of SRVan Travels' booking system, users are able to construct their own 1-day itinerary. This feature utilizes the Google Maps API, allowing users to click on points in the map for their itinerary. They are also provided with a list of popular tourist spots.

## Profile
Users are able to view their booking history and download a formatted PDF containing their itinerary/package details.
